Makes a corse grid and scans it then it makes finer and finer grids around the best points found.

  • Grids are deterministic
  • Algorithm stops when sqrt{ sum [ (p-p0) / p0 ]} < prec where p is the best value found in he current iteration and p0 in the precedent the sum is on the values of all the variables in "vars".
